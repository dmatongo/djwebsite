FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of events do you DJ for?

We provide DJ services for a variety of events including weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and more.

2. Do you take song requests?

Absolutely! We believe in creating a personalized experience and are happy to accommodate song requests.

3. How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, but ideally at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure availability.

4. What kind of equipment do you use?

We use professional-grade sound and lighting equipment to ensure the best quality for your event.

5. What is your cancellation policy?

For cancellations made more than 30 days before the event, we offer a full refund. For cancellations made less than 30 days before the event, the deposit is non-refundable.

6. Do you provide a written contract?

Yes, we provide a written contract detailing the services to be provided, the cost, and other important details.

7. Can you provide music for our ceremony and reception?

Yes, we can provide music for both your ceremony and reception. We can also accommodate special requests for specific songs or types of music.

8. How do you handle song transitions?

We seamlessly mix songs to ensure there are no awkward silences or abrupt transitions.

9. Do you have backup equipment?

Yes, we always bring backup equipment to ensure the event goes smoothly.

10. Do you offer a consultation before the event?

Yes, we offer a free consultation before the event to discuss your music preferences, event timeline, and other details.
